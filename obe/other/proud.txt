===== সংজ্ঞা: =====

The term "proud" or "prideful" refers to an attitude a person has when he thinks he is better or more important than someone else.

  * Pride often leads to disobeying God in other ways.
  * A proud person often does not admit his own faults. He is not humble.
  * Other English words that mean the same as proud are "arrogant," "conceited," and "self-important".
  * The terms "proud" and "pride" can also be used in a positive sense, such as being proud of someone who God has helped to overcome serious challenges in his life and is now successful in following God and blessing others.
  * The term "prideful" however is always negative, with the meaning of being arrogant or conceited.

(আরও দেখুন: নম্র, নম্রতা )
